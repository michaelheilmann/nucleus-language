Lexical

Nyx Language ignores ignores spaces (including new lines) and comments between lexical elements (words), except as delimiters between names and keywords.

Names (also called identifiers) in Nyx language can be any non-empty string of letters, digits, and underscores, not beginning with a digit and not being a reserved word. Names are used to form qualified names which is a sequence of one or more names where every two consecutive names are separated by a period.

Directives are qualified names with a leading period.
